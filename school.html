Our ICT Focus

At Zhong Ying School, we believe that technology is the language of the future. Our modern computing labs empower students to not just consume digital content, but to create it. We integrate advanced information and communication technology into our core curriculum from early stages.

Every student is unique. We provide a supportive, creative, and safe environment where critical thinking and problem-solving are prioritized. Our students are trained to be globally competitive while staying deeply connected to Cambodian values.

Our Vision

To be a premier educational institution in Cambodia that bridges traditional knowledge with high-tech computational skills, paving the way for sustainable digital development across the country.
